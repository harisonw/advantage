# DESIGN

## Theme

Light theme. Optimized for payout admins working daytime on standard office monitors.

## Brand palette (derived from provided logo assets)

- Brand navy: `#141b4d`
- Brand blue: `#2e71fe`

Neutrals should be slightly tinted, not pure white/black.

## Typography

- Use system sans stack.
- Strong hierarchy (clear H1/H2, compact body text).

## Layout

- Top nav with logo (left), deal id + overall status (right).
- Two-column workspace: check list (left) and details/evidence (right).
- Passed checks collapsed by default.

## Components

- Primary button: solid brand blue.
- Secondary button: outline or soft navy.
- Status chips: accessible success/warning/error tones; keep chroma restrained.

## Interaction

- Keyboard-friendly focus states.
- No modals by default; keep actions inline.

## Don’ts

- No extra pages or flows beyond the single-deal workspace.
- Avoid heavy shadows, glass effects, gradient text.
